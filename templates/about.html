About the Project

Our project is designed to predict potential diseases based on user-reported symptoms. By utilizing machine learning algorithms, the system can analyze these symptoms and predict possible illnesses with accuracy.

Beyond prediction, our platform offers valuable insights to manage and mitigate these health conditions. It provides:

  • Medication Guidance: Information on suitable medications that can help manage or treat the identified disease.
  • Workout Routines: Customized exercise plans designed to improve overall health and assist in recovery.
  • Diet Recommendations: Nutritional advice tailored to support immune function and speed recovery.
  • Precautionary Measures: Effective steps users can take to prevent further health risks and complications.
  • Detailed Disease Information: Comprehensive insights about the predicted condition, including causes, symptoms, and treatment methods.

Our goal is to empower individuals to take control of their health by providing reliable information and guidance for better well-being. With this system, we strive to bridge the gap between healthcare accessibility and preventive care.
